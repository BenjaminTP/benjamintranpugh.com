## Overview

Nordic hamstring curl equipment costs hundreds of dollars I didn't have to spare. So I designed and built my own bench from wood salvaged from an old bed frame and spare household materials. Total cost was under $50.

<Figure src="nordic-bench/live.png" caption="THE FINISHED BENCH — PADDED ANKLE BAR AND GRIP DECK" />

## Build

<Figure src="nordic-bench/cad.png" align="right" caption="ONSHAPE MODEL" />

I modeled the bench in Onshape to work out geometry and loads, then built it with hand saws, wood joinery, and fasteners.

## Result

A functional Nordic bench that holds up to daily use, at a fraction of retail price.

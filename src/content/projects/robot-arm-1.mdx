## Overview

This was my first robotic arm. I started with no experience in electronics, programming, and minimal experience in CAD. Everything was built from scratch, through a lot of iterations and failures, until it worked.

<Figure src="robot-arm-1/live.png" caption="ARM, BREADBOARDS, AND JOYSTICK CONTROL" />

## Build

<Figure src="robot-arm-1/cad.png" align="right" caption="ONSHAPE MODEL" />

The arm was modeled in Onshape and printed on a Bambu Lab printer. An Arduino running C++ drives servo and stepper motors through motor drivers, with joysticks and buttons for control.

## Result

A working arm, a foundation in electronics and embedded C++, and the design experience that made [Robot Arm 2.0](/projects/robot-arm-2/) possible.

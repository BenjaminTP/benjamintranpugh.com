## Overview

This was my first robotic arm — started with no experience in electronics and minimal experience in CAD. Everything was built from scratch, through a lot of iterations and failures, until it worked.

<Figure src="robot-arm-1/hero.jpg" caption="ROBOT ARM 1.0, COMPLETE" />

## Build

The arm was modeled in Onshape and printed on a Bambu Lab printer. An Arduino running C++ drives servo and stepper motors through motor drivers, with joysticks and buttons for control.

<Figure src="robot-arm-1/electronics.jpg" align="right" caption="ARDUINO AND DRIVER WIRING" />

## Result

A working arm, a foundation in electronics and embedded C++, and the design experience that made [Robot Arm 2.0](/projects/robot-arm-2/) possible.
